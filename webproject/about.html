What is PeerConnect ?
and how does it help students?

PeerConnect is a smart student-to-student mentoring platform designed for AUSI University. It connects learners who need help in specific courses with fellow students who are strong in those subjects. Each student starts with 10 free coins, and sessions cost 5 coins. Mentors earn coins by offering help, and they can use them later to request mentoring themselves.
